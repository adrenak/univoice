    SegmentRate

    The number of segments (a segment is a sequence of audio samples) that are emitted from the source every second. A 16000 Hz source with one channel at a rate of 10 will output an array of 1600 samples every 100 milliseconds. A 44000 Hz source with two channels at a rate of 10 will output an array of 8800 samples every 100 milliseconds.
